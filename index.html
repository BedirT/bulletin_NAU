FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the goal of this app?

Our goal is to help students and faculty members to easily communicate and avoid those lame emails and short office hours.

Can I share files using this app?

Not yet. We are currently working on adding more features which would help sharing files and more.

Is there a Android version?

Not yet. We will port Bulletin to Android a bit later in the year.

I found a bug, whom do I report this to?

Please open an issue in our Github repository and we will get on it ASAP.
